Integrated AI Services Disclosure

1. Our Philosophy

Vision Education utilizes state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence to provide students with a premium learning experience. We believe in transparency regarding how AI is used, ensuring that our students and educators understand the technology powering our platform.

2. Integrated AI Services

We have integrated powerful AI models to enhance your academic preparation. Specifically, we use:

GPT-4.0

Powers our advanced theory marking engine, providing logical breakdown, step-by-step feedback, and scoring on student workings, simulating a real examiner's assessment.

Gemini 2.5

Assists in content curation, intelligent question generation, and ensuring all practice materials and feedback align accurately with the latest WAEC curriculum standards.

3. Data Privacy and AI

Your privacy is paramount. Data sent to these AI services is completely anonymized and used solely for the purpose of generating educational feedback in real-time. No personally identifiable information (PII) is transmitted to these AI models, and your data is never used to train external public models.

4. Limitations and Guarantees

While our AI models provide highly accurate feedback and grading, they are designed as supplementary educational tools. They emulate WAEC marking schemes to the best of their capabilities but do not replace official WAEC examiners. Always consult with your teachers and official syllabuses.
