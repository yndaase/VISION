Platform Overview

How Vision Education Works

A structured, four-step preparation process designed to build genuine exam competence.

Step 01
Choose Your Subject

Log in to your secure dashboard and select any of the nine available WASSCE subjects. Questions load instantly, filtered by the most recent syllabus.

Step 02
Real-World Contexts

Questions are embedded in authentic Ghanaian scenarios — Mobile Money transactions, ECG utility billing, agricultural yields — making abstract concepts immediately applicable.

Step 03
Instant Examiner Feedback

Every answer — correct or incorrect — triggers a full explanation of the working steps and the Chief Examiner's reasoning for each distractor option.

Step 04
Full Data Privacy

All session data and scores are stored entirely within your browser's local storage. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or sold — ever.

Our Principles

What We Stand For

Three commitments that define every decision we make on the platform.

Academic Accuracy

Every question is reviewed against the official WAEC 2026 syllabus. Distractor logic is based on documented common student errors.

Zero Data Collection

We do not store, track, or profile users. Your practice sessions remain private, stored only in your own browser.

Immediate Utility

No registration required to begin. Students can access practice questions within seconds of visiting the platform.
