How It Works

1. Select Subject

Choose Your Focus

From your secure dashboard, click on any subject you wish to study. The platform instantly loads a curated set of multiple-choice questions matched exactly to the most recent WAEC syllabus.

2. Contextual Questions

Real-World Contexts

Gone are the days of boring formulas. You will encounter questions dealing with Mobile Money (MoMo) transfer rates, ECG utility billing, Fintech investments, and Agricultural yields.

3. Instant Clarification

Instant Examiner Feedback

As soon as you select an answer, whether right or wrong, the platform shows you the complete mathematical workings. Better yet, it reveals the Chief Examiner's logic on why certain "trap" answers trick students.

4. Deep Privacy

100% Local Data

Your accounts and scores are absolutely secure. The database lives entirely inside your local browser storage. We never collect, sell, or monitor your personal data. Practice completely anonymously.
