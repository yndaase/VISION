Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes! The free plan is completely free with no time limit. You get 50 AI questions per day, which is perfect for casual learning. No credit card required.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely! You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your dashboard. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods through Paystack including Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfers.
Do you offer student discounts?
Yes! Contact us at support@visionedu.online with your student ID for special pricing. We also offer group discounts for schools.
What happens if I exceed the free plan limit?
If you reach the 50 questions per day limit on the free plan, you'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro for unlimited access. Your limit resets every 24 hours.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade from Free to Pro anytime. If you downgrade from Pro to Free, you'll keep Pro features until the end of your billing period.
