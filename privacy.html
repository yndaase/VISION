Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

1. Overview

Vision Education (the "Service", "we", "us") is a non-profit educational platform developed by Yaw Ndaase Mensuoh for the benefit of Ghanaian Senior High School students. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your student data is handled securely and transparently.

2. Data & Usage

We employ a "privacy-by-design" approach. Most of your practice data (scores, answers, and progress) is stored locally in your browser's Local Storage and is never transmitted to our servers unless explicitly synced for cross-device access.

Google OAuth Disclosure

If you sign in using your Google Account, we request access to your:

  • Email address — Used exclusively to identify your account and save your progress across devices.
  • Profile information — Used to personalise your Student Dashboard and academic reports.

3. Google API Policy

Vision Education's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not use your Google user data for advertising, nor do we sell your information to third-party data brokers.

4. Biometric Data

To verify identity and unlock premium features, we offer Face Verification powered by Face++ (Megvii). When you capture a selfie, it is processed securely via their API to detect human presence and verify face uniqueness. We do not store raw biometric face maps — only a "verified status" flag and timestamp in our secure Firebase database.

5. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated Google user data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will purge all identifiable records within 30 days.
