Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vision Education. We are committed to protecting the privacy of Ghanaian Senior High School students using our platform to prepare for the WASSCE exams. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

Vision Education primarily uses Local Storage, meaning your practice scores and session data are stored directly in your browser and never sent to our servers. However, if you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Identity Data: Your name and email address provided by Google OAuth.
  • Profile Picture: To personalize your student dashboard.

3. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide a personalized examination practice experience.
  • Track your subject mastery and AI-powered diagnostic progress.
  • Secure your account against unauthorized access.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Google OAuth Disclosure

Vision Education's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your account. For our Robotics division, we utilize end-to-end encryption (E2EE) for communications.
