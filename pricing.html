Frequently asked questions

Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes — the Standard plan is permanently free. You get access to Core Mathematics practice questions, mock exams, and Gemini AI assistance with no expiry date and no credit card required.
What is the difference between GPT-4o and Gemini 2.5?
The free plan uses Gemini 2.5 for AI assistance. Vision Pro unlocks Azure GPT-4o — our Copilot engine — which provides more granular Section B marking, step-by-step feedback and Chief Examiner-style commentary on your theory answers.
How does Enterprise pricing work?
Enterprise is priced annually based on student seat count. Email us at mensuohyaw@gmail.com with your school name and estimated number of students and we will send a tailored quote within 24 hours.
Can I upgrade or cancel at any time?
Yes. Pro is a monthly subscription — you can cancel from your account settings at any time before the next billing date. Your access continues until the end of the paid period.
